# Market Entry Playbook for a New Operations Segment

## Problem

The business wanted to expand into a new segment, but the decision lacked a framework that compared opportunity size, economics, and execution risk in one place.

## Context / Role

Owned the analysis and recommendation narrative, converting raw assumptions into a leadership-ready decision memo.

## Solution / Approach

- Modeled three entry scenarios across market size, staffing footprint, and contribution margin.
- Split reversible experiments from harder commitments.
- Turned the recommendation into a phased operator playbook.

## Impact

- Leadership reviewed three clear entry paths instead of one abstract expansion idea.
- The recommended phased model projected 14 percent margin upside.
- The team adopted a staged plan rather than a full-risk launch.

## Key Learnings

- Strategy is easier to trust when it shows sequencing, not just destination.
- Decision quality improves when analysis makes risk and learning explicit.
